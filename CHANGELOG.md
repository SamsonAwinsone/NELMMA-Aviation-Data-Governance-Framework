## [1.0.0] — 2025 (Initial Release)

### Added
- README.md — Complete project overview and framework documentation
- BUSINESS_CASE.md — Full business justification with ROI framing
- GOVERNANCE_CHARTER.md — Formal governance charter document
- Governance Operating Model — Hierarchy, RACI, decision rights, escalation paths
- Policy Suite (DG-POL-001 to DG-POL-008):
  - Data Classification Policy
  - Access Control Policy
  - Data Retention and Disposal Policy
  - Privacy and Consent Policy
  - Data Quality Policy
  - Breach Response Policy
  - Cross-Border Data Transfer Policy
  - Metadata Management Policy
- Portfolio content: Case study, LinkedIn description, resume bullets, interview talking points
- GitHub best practices and repository checklist
- .gitignore for sensitive data protection

### Framework Scope
- Organization: Nelmma Aviation, Ghana
- Markets: Nigeria, Liberia, Mali, China, Belgium
- Standards: DAMA-DMBOK, ISO 38505, ISO 27001, ISO 8000, Act 843, GDPR, PIPL
- Implementation timeline: 6-phase plan spanning Year 1–6
